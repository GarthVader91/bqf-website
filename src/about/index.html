What is Bible Quizzing

Bible quizzing is an exciting way to get students into the Word of God, and thus the Word of God into students. With a rotating schedule of material, quizzers are exposed to a large percentage of the New Testament. Competing locally and nationally, quizzers typically memorize seven or more chapters of scripture each season, and in competition learn how to work as a team, think on their feet, and handle pressure.

What is Bible Quiz Fellowship?

The Bible Quiz Fellowship exists to support, encourage, and knit together existing quiz ministries and to promote, help establish, and equip new quiz programs throughout the United States. BQF also facilitates Regional Tournaments as well as a National Tournament and maintains a website designed to actively communicate the fundamental purpose of BQF: to get students into the Word and thus the Word into students.
